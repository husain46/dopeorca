FAQ

Common questions
answered

How much does a website cost? +

Pricing depends on scope, complexity, and requirements. A basic business website starts from ₹25,000 – ₹50,000. Custom web applications and larger projects are quoted based on a detailed brief. We provide transparent, itemized quotes with no hidden charges.

How long does it take to complete a project? +

Most websites are delivered in 2–4 weeks. Landing pages in 3–7 business days. Complex software products take 6–12 weeks. We agree on a realistic timeline at the start of every project and stick to it.

Do you work with international clients? +

Yes — we actively serve clients in UAE, UK, Singapore, and Canada. Our remote-first workflow handles time zone differences smoothly. All project communication happens in English, and we accept international payments.

Do I need to know exactly what I want before contacting you? +

Not at all. Many of our best projects start with just a general idea. Our discovery process is designed to help you clarify your requirements, goals, and priorities — so even if you're at the "I think I need a website" stage, reach out.

What happens after my website launches? +

Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. Beyond that, we offer monthly maintenance packages for ongoing updates, security, and performance monitoring. We're built to be a long-term partner, not a one-off vendor.

How do I verify an intern's participation in DopeOrca? +

Email us at dopeorca7@gmail.com with the intern's full name, college, and your institution's official request. We respond with formal verification documentation within 2–3 business days. Internship certificates are issued upon program completion.
