About The Program

Not a simulation.
Real work, real clients.

The DopeOrca Internship Program is a structured, supervised experience where participants work on actual client projects — not dummy assignments or sandbox environments. Every deliverable ships to a real business.

Interns are matched to their domain of interest and assigned a senior mentor who guides their work, reviews deliverables, and provides structured feedback throughout the program.

  • ✓ Structured weekly learning objectives and milestones
  • ✓ 1-on-1 weekly mentorship sessions
  • ✓ Portfolio-ready deliverables you can showcase
  • ✓ Formal internship certificate on completion
  • ✓ Opportunity to convert to a paid role

For Colleges & Universities

Official internship
documentation

DopeOrca Technologies provides all documentation required by educational institutions for internship credit — including offer letters, mid-program reports, and completion certificates.

All internship records are maintained formally and can be verified by placement coordinators, faculty advisors, or college administrations upon formal request.

✓ Verification Process
For educational institutions needing to verify a student's internship with DopeOrca Technologies:
  • 1 Email dopeorca7@gmail.com with student's name, roll number, and institution
  • 2 Attach official institution letterhead or request form
  • 3 Receive formal verification letter within 2–3 business days
  • 4 Completion certificates issued digitally with unique ID
