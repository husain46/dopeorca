Our Story

We're not an agency.
We're your tech team.

DopeOrca Technologies was founded in 2025 in Mumbai with a single conviction: every serious business deserves a serious digital presence — without paying enterprise prices or waiting months for delivery.

We saw too many businesses — salons, restaurants, clinics, startups — losing customers because their digital presence didn't match the quality of their service. Overpriced agencies. Template-based freelancers. Zero accountability post-launch. We set out to fix that.

Today, DopeOrca Technologies operates across four global markets, delivering custom websites, software, and AI-powered solutions to businesses that are serious about growth. We're not the biggest. We don't want to be. We want to be the best partner our clients ever had.
