About the Company

Built to make
businesses unforgettable

DopeOrca Technologies is a Mumbai-based digital solutions company founded by Shahid Khan in 2023. We specialize in website design and development, UI/UX design, AI automation, SEO, and custom business software.

We were founded on a single belief: that every business — regardless of size or budget — deserves a world-class digital presence. Since launch, we've served clients across India, UAE, the UK, and Singapore, completing 50+ projects with 100% client satisfaction.

We are not a freelancer collective or a bloated agency. We are a lean, focused technology company that combines startup speed with enterprise-grade standards — delivering results that genuinely move the needle for our clients' businesses.
