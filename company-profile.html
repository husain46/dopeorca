🏢 Company Overview

DopeOrca Technologies is a Mumbai-based Information Technology company specializing in the design, development, and deployment of websites, software products, AI automation systems, and complete digital solutions. We serve clients across India and internationally, helping businesses at every stage build a powerful digital presence and operate more efficiently through technology.

Founded with a mission to democratize access to enterprise-grade digital solutions, DopeOrca combines deep technical expertise with genuine business understanding to deliver products that create measurable impact. Our team includes developers, designers, and AI specialists committed to excellence, transparency, and continuous innovation.

🎓 Internship Program & Verification

DopeOrca Technologies operates a structured internship program open to students from colleges and universities across India. Interns work on live client projects under the guidance of senior team members, gaining real-world experience in Web Development, UI/UX Design, SEO, AI Automation, and Business Operations.
