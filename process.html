How We Work

From idea to launch
in 8 structured steps

A transparent, proven process that keeps every project on track, on budget, and on time — with no surprises along the way.

01
Phase 1
Discover

We start by deeply understanding your business — your goals, your audience, your competition, and the specific problem you're trying to solve. This isn't a checkbox exercise; it's the foundation that makes every subsequent decision smarter.

Discovery Call Business Brief Goal Alignment
02
Phase 2
Research

We analyse your competitors, study your target users, and benchmark against industry standards. This research phase ensures we're not designing in a vacuum — every choice is backed by data and real user insight.

Competitor Analysis User Research Market Benchmarking
03
Phase 3
Strategy

We create a clear project roadmap: information architecture, technology stack decisions, content strategy, and KPIs. You'll know exactly what we're building, why, and how we'll measure success before a single pixel is designed.

Project Roadmap Tech Stack Decision KPI Framework Sitemap
04
Phase 4
Design

Wireframes first, then high-fidelity designs — with your input at every stage. We don't disappear for two weeks and return with a finished product. Design is a collaborative process, and your feedback shapes the final look and feel.

Wireframes UI Design (Figma) Design Review Brand Alignment
05
Phase 5
Develop

Clean, documented code — no page builders, no bloated plugins. We build to web performance standards: fast load times, semantic HTML, accessible markup, and mobile-first responsiveness built in from the ground up.

Frontend Development Backend / CMS Integrations Performance Optimisation
06
Phase 6
Test

Every project goes through rigorous testing: cross-browser compatibility, device testing across phones and tablets, performance audits (Lighthouse scores), form and integration testing, and a full content review. Nothing ships with known issues.

Cross-Browser Testing Mobile QA Lighthouse Audit Content Review
07
Phase 7
Launch

We handle the full deployment — domain configuration, SSL, hosting setup, DNS propagation, and go-live checks. You get a smooth, stress-free launch with us managing every technical detail so you can focus on your business.

Deployment SSL & Domain Setup Analytics Go-Live Launch Checklist
08
Phase 8
Support & Grow

The relationship doesn't end at launch. We provide structured post-launch support, monitor performance metrics, fix any issues that emerge, and work with you on ongoing improvements. Most of our clients become long-term partners.

Post-Launch Support Performance Monitoring Ongoing Retainer Growth Roadmap
